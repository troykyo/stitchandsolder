Tell us what you do

The end of the evening is dedcated to what you do, Bring a project to share.

  • Pattern Cutting

    Do you unfold mesh geometry or 3D body scan to make tailored garments? Come cut it up.

  • Textile Nerdery

    Hacked a knitting machine? Encode secret messages in the weave? Build your own spinning frames? Take us for a spin.

  • Future Design

    Are you designing the future aesthetics of wearables? Show us what it looks like.

  • VR/AR/MR

    Is the digital interface your means of expression? Let us see through your eyes

  • Showing Emotion

    Is your heartbeat public? Do you clothes change to your mood? Feel us your story.

  • Wearing Sound

    Is Sound just another fabric? Do you wrap yourself in beats? Let us hear it.
